Why Shelf Share?

Shelf Share is your community-driven board game library. Discover new favorites, connect with other fans, and share the fun. Whether you’re a casual gamer or a passionate collector, we make it simple to borrow and lend games through your local library system.

How It Works

1. Browse Games

Explore our community collection of board games and find one you love.

2. Request to Borrow

Submit a borrow request, wait for approval, and come to one of three easy pickup locations at UVA!

3. Enjoy & Return

Pick up your game, have fun, and then easily return it when you’re done.
